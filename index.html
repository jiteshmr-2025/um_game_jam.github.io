Mission Briefing

Welcome to the UM Game Jam. You have 48 hours to craft a game from scratch based on a secret theme. Whether you are a coding wizard, a pixel artist, or a sound engineer, your skills are needed in the server.

This event is open to all students and public developers. Bring your laptop, your charger, and your creativity.

Jam Tracks

Focus on technical complexity and innovative code mechanics. Break the engine limits.

Pixel Painters

Best audio design and original soundtrack. Make our ears happy.
