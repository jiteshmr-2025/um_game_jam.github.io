Server Handbook

I. The Mission

UM Game Jam 2026 is a 4-day hybrid game development hackathon organized by Persatuan Komputer (PEKOM). It consists of three (3) days online and one (1) physical day.

Your goal is to craft a playable game from scratch based on a secret theme. The official language for communication, pitching, and judging is English.

The theme will be revealed during the official briefing on 15th April 2026 at 9:45 PM.

II. Eligibility & Party Setup

  • > Who Can Join: All undergraduate students aged 18 and above from any public or private university in Malaysia.
  • > Party Size: Solo adventurers or teams of 1 to 4 members. Cross-university teams are highly encouraged!
  • > No Respecs: Team configurations cannot be changed after registration is submitted.

III. Development Rules & Assets

  • > Engines: All game engines (including no-code) are permitted. External hosted platforms (e.g., Roblox) are NOT allowed.
  • > Assets: Pre-made assets are permitted as long as they are legally licensed, credited, and disclosed.
  • > Generative AI: AI tools are allowed but must be disclosed. Projects heavily reliant on AI-generated assets may receive a light deduction during judging.
  • > Intellectual Property: You own what you make! All IP rights remain with the participants.

IV. Submissions & Evaluation

Teams must submit one game via an itch.io project page as a ZIP file containing either:

  • A web-based HTML5/WebGL build (playable in-browser).
  • A standalone desktop build (Windows .exe or macOS .app).

Pitching: Teams have up to 4 minutes to pitch and demonstrate the game, followed by a Q&A session. Judges may play the game during this time.

Criteria: Games are evaluated equally (solo vs. team) on Creativity, Originality, Gameplay, and Technical Stability.

V. Loot Table (Prizes)

★ Grand Winner (Cash Prize + Award)

★ Runner Up (Cash Prize + Award)

★ Second Runner Up (Cash Prize + Award)

+ All Participants: Receive an official E-Certificate of Participation.
