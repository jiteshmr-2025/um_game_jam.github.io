Server Handbook

I. The Mission

The UM Game Jam 2026 is the premier game development hackathon. The "Server" is open to students from all faculties and universities—diversity is our strength.

Your goal: Craft a playable game from scratch based on a specific theme. The theme will be revealed online on 15th April 2026 at 9:45 PM.

II. Server Rules

  • > Party Size: Solo adventurers or parties of 1 to 5 players.
  • > The Quest (Theme): You cannot choose your own theme. You must adhere to the organizers' theme revealed at the start.
  • > Side Quests (Challenges): 3 Optional Challenges will be released in stages. Completing them grants bonus points!
  • > AI Policy: Generative AI tools are permitted but must be disclosed in your submission log.
