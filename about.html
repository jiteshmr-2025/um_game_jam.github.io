Server Handbook

I. The Mission

The UM Game Jam is the premier game development hackathon at Universiti Malaya. For 48 hours, the "Server" is open for developers, artists, and musicians to collaborate.

Your goal is simple: Craft a playable game from scratch based on a theme revealed at the start of the event. No previous experience required—just a laptop and your creativity.

II. Server Rules

  • > Team Size: Solo adventurers or Parties of up to 4 players.
  • > Assets: Premade engines (Unity/Godot) are allowed. Art and Audio must be original creations.
  • > AI Policy: Generative AI tools are permitted but must be disclosed in your submission log.

III. Loot Table (Prizes)

★ Grand Winner: RM 1,500 + Mentorship

★ Runner Up: RM 800 + Peripherals

★ Second Runner Up: RM 400
